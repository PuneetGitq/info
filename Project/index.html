ABOUT COLLEGE



Our College is a unique educational institution of North India and holds a pre-eminent position in the glorious past of this region. In 1916 the institution was set up in Lahore, under the dynamic leadership of Bharat Ratna Mahamana Madan Mohan Malviya Ji, the backbone of Sanatan Dharma. After the partition of the country, the college was re-established in Ambala and the foundation stone of the building was laid by none other than India's first President Dr. Rajender Prasad on 3 October 1951.
